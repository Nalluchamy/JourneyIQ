# JourneyIQ Dashboard Guide
### A Plain-Language User Guide for Shop Owners and Staff

Welcome to JourneyIQ! This dashboard is designed to help you run your store without getting bogged down by complicated technical terms or math equations. Here is what each section of your dashboard does, in plain English.

---

## 1. Home (Overview)
- **What it is:** The main control center showing how your store is doing today.
- **What it shows:**
  - **Revenue:** The total money you've made from sales today.
  - **Orders:** The number of completed checkouts.
  - **Active Sessions:** The number of people currently clicking around or browsing your store.
  - **Stock Alerts:** Flags items that are running low or completely sold out.
- **What to look for:** Look at the color indicators: 🟢 means everything is healthy, 🟡 means worth keeping an eye on, and 🔴 means you need to restock items right away.

## 2. Customers
- **What it is:** A simple breakdown of the different groups of people visiting your shop.
- **What it shows:**
  - **Active VIP Shoppers:** Your most loyal, big-spending customers who buy frequently.
  - **Slipping / At Risk:** Customers who might stop buying because they haven't visited in a while.
  - **New Registrations:** People who just signed up for an account.
  - **Quiet / Inactive:** Dormant accounts that haven't ordered in a long time.
- **Suggested Action:** Under "Slipping / At Risk", the dashboard will suggest sending these customers a coupon or special promotion to bring them back!

## 3. Sales Summary
- **What it is:** A plain-language translation of your billing and transaction reports.
- **What it shows:**
  - Total revenue made during the selected time period.
  - Total number of successful orders.
  - Average order value (how much a customer spends on average per order).
  - Payment success rates and what percentage of orders used a coupon.
- **Detailed Logs:** If you want to see the spreadsheet of raw data, click **"View Detailed Transaction Logs"** to see invoices, customer names, dates, and order statuses.

## 4. AI Insights
- **What it is:** Automated recommendations written in plain sentences showing how to improve your sales.
- **What it shows:** Helpful tips generated by our AI model based on real customer behavior.
- **Suggested Action:** Each card gives you a clear **"Suggested Action"** (e.g., "Increase order quantity for this item" or "Run a bundle promotion on slow items") so you know exactly what to do next.

## 5. Model Performance
- **What it is:** A status screen showing how well the AI recommendation model is helping you personalize your store.
- **What it shows:**
  - **Current Model:** The version of the recommendation engine running.
  - **Last Trained:** When the model last updated its training weights.
  - **Inference Time:** How fast the AI recommends items to a customer (usually in milliseconds).
  - A simple comparison grid showing how much better the Deep Learning (NCF) model performs compared to standard popular filters.

## 6. Settings
- **What it is:** The place to configure your store's basic preferences.
- **What it shows:**
  - **Storefront Name & Email:** Customize your platform identity.
  - **Theme & Language:** Toggle between different dark modes and languages (English, Spanish, French).
  - **Notification & Backup Options:** Configure email alerts for low stock and automatic daily database backups.
